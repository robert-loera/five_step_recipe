Step 1: In a mixing bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder and baking soda

Step 2: In another bowl, combine milk, lemon juice, ad egg yolk. Add dry flour mixture and stir gently. Do not over mix.

Step 3: Beat egg white until stiff peaks form, add sugar and blend for a second longer.

Step 4: Gently fold in egg whites into flour/egg mixture, paying special care not to over mic

Step 5: Cook in a non-stick slightly greased waffle iron
